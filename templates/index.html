Setup Guide
UP AND RUNNING IN
THREE STEPS.
No complex configuration. Sign in once, install the client, then just play normally — data flows automatically.
01Connect with Discord
Sign In Once.
Click Open Dashboard above. Authorize The Dispatch with your Discord account — your Discord ID becomes your fleet identity. No extra accounts, no passwords.
→ Click "Open Dashboard" → Authorize with Discord → Create or join a VTC → Your fleet is ready
02Install the Client
Download. Run. Done.
Download the Windows installer. On first run it opens a Discord login, finds your save files automatically, and installs the SCS telemetry plugin — no manual configuration needed.
→ Download TheDispatch.exe → Run — sign in with Discord → Client sits in system tray → Watching save files...
03Drive. Monitor. Repeat.
Just Play the Game.
Fire up ATS or ETS2 and drive normally. Every job completion is detected and logged in seconds. Open the dashboard anytime to see live status, history, and standings.
